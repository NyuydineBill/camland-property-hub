CamLand User Account Features
CamLand User Account can exist as an unverified account or a verified account and can be
created by any member of the public. Upon creation the account exists as an unverified
account but can be verified if the user wishes to do so. All CamLand accounts must have a
unique ID for better security.
Unverified User Account:
An unverified user account has the following privileges:
• View all property listings
• Engage commercial listings on House and Land Rents, Hotels, Guest House, and
Eateries
• Request for property verification status for other properties.
On the other hand, an unverified user account has the following limitations
• Can only see House and Land sale listings but cannot engage them. User will be
required to verify their accounts to continue
• Cannot register any newly acquired real estate property
Verified User Account:
A verified user account has the following privileges:
• View all property listings
• Engage all commercial listings
• Request for property verification status for other properties
• Register any newly acquired real estate property or receive property transferred to
their accounts
• Upon payment of a property engagement fee, the user has the possibility to delegate
powers to another user of a verified CamLand Account to mediate the acquisition of a
new real estate property of theirs on their behalf.
NB: A user account can be upgraded to any CamLand account type (Owner Account,
Community Head Account and Broker Account) and equally any CamLand Account type can
be downgraded to a user account. For example, in the case where a user with a user account
acquires a real estate property and register’s it to their account by providing the necessary
documents, the user account is automatically upgraded to a user account upon validation of
the submitted documents by CamLand and in the case where an owner with an owner’s
account sale their property and has no other property registered to his/her account, the
account is automatically downgraded to a user account. So, all accounts can be downgraded
to a user account.
To verify a user account, the user needs to provide CamLand with the following information:
Personal Information:
Name
Email (to be verified)
Contact(s) (to be verified)
NID or Passport Number
Picture of Identification Document
Selfie Picture
CamLand Owner Account Features
A CamLand Owner Account can either be created upon registration of account or by
upgrading a User account upon registration of a property, hence available only for property
owners. This account MUST be verified and have a property registered to their accounts for it
to exist. An Owner’s account has the following privileges:
• View all property listings
• Engage all commercial listings
• Register any newly acquired real estate property or receive property transferred to
their accounts
• Upon payment of a property engagement fee, the user has the possibility to delegate
powers to another user of a verified CamLand Account to mediate the acquisition of a
new real estate property of theirs on their behalf.
• Request for property verification status for their property and other properties
• Give consent about other owners registering their properties near theirs and earn
commissions (Vouching) based on their credit score.
• Delegate powers to another user with a verified CamLand account to mediate the sale
of their properties on the behalf.
• List their property as private or commercial
• Initiate an ownership transfer request
To register a property to CamLand, the Owner must provide CamLand with the following
information:
Personal Information:
Name
Email (to be verified)
Contact(s) (to be verified)
NID or Passport Number
Picture of Identification Document
Selfie
Property Information:
Region
Division
Sub-Division
Quarter
Property Dimensions
Geographical Coordinate
Property Document (Upload Scanned Copy)
Property Status
Pictures of the Property
Next of Kin Information (Optional):
Name
NID or Passport
Phone Number
Picture
Picture of Identification Document
Scanned Copy of Birth Certificate
NB: Optionally decision about property in case of demise
Owner must accept CamLand terms and conditions in order for their registration to go through
(Terms and Conditions to be developed). Upon registration the said property is listed against
the owner account and is assigned a unique property ID by CamLand.
NB: More than one property can be registered under on account but one property cannot be
registered to more than one account unless in the case of inheritance and the specificities
governing the will, court order or any relevant legal document must be highlighted upon
registration and such properties cannot be sold without the consent of both parties.
CamLand Community Head Account Features
A CamLand Community Head Account can either be created upon registration of account or
by upgrading a User account upon provision of authentic supporting documents and
validation by CamLand. This account can only be owned by community heads like “Quarter
Heads”, Fons/Chiefs, MINCAF’s Sub-Divisional Representatives, and Council delegates.
This account must be verified and has the following privileges:
• View all property listings
• Engage all commercial listings
• Register any newly acquired real estate property or receive property transferred to
their accounts
• Upon payment of a property engagement fee, the user has the possibility to delegate
powers to another user of a verified CamLand Account to mediate the acquisition of a
new real estate property of theirs on their behalf.
• Request for property verification status for their property (if user owns a real estate
property) and other properties
• Delegate powers to another user with a verified CamLand account to mediate the sale
of their properties on the behalf (If user owns a real estate property)
• List their property as private or commercial (If user owns a real estate property)
• Initiate an ownership transfer request (If user owns a real estate property)
• Give consent about other owners registering their properties in their community and
earn commissions (Vouching) based on their credit score.
NB: A Community Head Account Owner can equally have their properties registered to their
accounts such that they use one account for everything.
The documents needed by CamLand to validate a Community Account creation request is
letter of designation or recognition by a competent authority.
CamLand Broker’s Account Features
A CamLand Broker Account can either be created upon registration of account or by
upgrading a User account upon provision of authentic supporting documents and validation
by CamLand. This account can only be owned by licensed real estate agents . This account
must be verified and has the following privileges:
• View all property listings
• Engage all commercial listings
• Register any newly acquired real estate property or receive property transferred to
their accounts
• Upon payment of a property engagement fee, the user has the possibility to delegate
powers to another user of a verified CamLand Account to mediate the acquisition of a
new real estate property of theirs on their behalf.
• Request for property verification status for their property (if user owns a real estate
property) and other properties
• Delegate powers to another user with a verified CamLand account to mediate the sale
of their properties on the behalf (If user owns a real estate property)
• List their property as private or commercial (If user owns a real estate property)
• Initiate an ownership transfer request (If user owns a real estate property)
• Give consent about other owners registering their properties in their community and
earn commissions (Vouching) based on their credit score.
• Register properties on behalf of owner’s an earn a commission
• List other property owner’s properties as commercial upon approval by owner and
earn a commission upon sale of that property
NB: An Owner of a Broker Account can equally have their properties registered to their
accounts such that they use one account for everything.
The documents needed by CamLand to validate a Broker Account creation request is a real
estate agent license signed by a competent authority.
